How Do I Use The Website?

  1. 1. In the designated input field, enter the names of your dinner guests one by one. You can either type them directly into the field or copy and paste from another source.
  2. 2. After entering each guest's name, you can click on the "Add Guest" button to add them to the list. Repeat this process for each guest you want to invite.
  3. 3. Take a moment to review your guest list. If you need to make any changes, such as adding or removing someone from the list, you can do so at this stage.
  4. 4. After saving or finalizing your guest list, you may receive a confirmation message indicating that your changes have been successfully processed.
